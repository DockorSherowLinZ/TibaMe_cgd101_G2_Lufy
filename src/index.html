About Light Up For You

Starting from six sketches in 1991, the founder Meiric sowed a design seed, carefully irrigated with delicate thinking and rigorous attitude, rubbed light and shadow into brush and ink, and outlined the lighting imagination in his heart. We firmly believe that "light" can bring more beautiful value to life, which is our original intention.

With the addition of more creative workers, the seeds have turned into fertile soil, and the branches and leaves of the sprouts are intertwined, creating more possibilities for each other. Lights, in addition to illuminating different daily scenes, play a role in drawing people and the field to live together. In the daily interaction with people and space, we share the light and shadow poems created for the ideal life.
