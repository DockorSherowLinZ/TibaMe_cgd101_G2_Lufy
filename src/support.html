Member

  • How to change member information and password?

    Please click "Member Information/Change Password" in "Member Center" after "Member Login" to make changes.

Order

  • Are the items in stock?

    Because both online and physical stores are selling goods at the same time, the flow of goods is fast. It is recommended to confirm the inventory status before placing an order to be more accurate.

  • Is the order successful?

    After the order is completed, you can go to "Member Center > Order List" to check whether the order is successful.

  • How to track order progress?

    For the shipment progress of the product, you can check it in "Member Center > Order List".

  • View my coupons?

    Go to "Member Center > Discount Coupon List" to check discount coupons.

  • How to use coupons?

    At checkout, click to use coupon to enjoy the discount!

  • Discount coupon usage rules

    • 1. For online checkout, each order is limited to one coupon, and the store usage rules will be announced separately.
    • 2. Discount coupons can only be used once without change, and cannot be recovered once used.
    • 3. Discount coupons have an expiration date, after which they cannot be used and cannot be restored.

After-sales

  • LUFY is there an installation service?

    Yes, LUFY has a construction team with more than ten years of experience. You can check whether you need a special person to install the service when you buy it. Since LUFY's lamps are all newly designed lamps, their shapes and structures are often different from ordinary lamps. Some plumbers in the market often cause assembly errors because they do not understand the structure of lamps. Therefore, we recommend that you buy design lamps in our store. The correct construction is entrusted to the LUFY professional team. If it is not constructed by LUFY, the lamps and lanterns and safety negligence are not covered by the warranty for damage or accidents caused by outsourced installation.

Delivery

  • Shipping method?

    Commodities are delivered to the house by specially-assigned persons.

  • When will the item be received?

    Your order will be processed immediately after payment is confirmed and you will receive the item on the specified date.

  • Can I change the shipping address or contact number?

    Please call the original purchase store to inquire. The store needs to further confirm whether it has been shipped. If it has been shipped, you need to receive it at the original address; if it has not shipped, the delivery address information can be modified for you.
