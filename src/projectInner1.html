LAMP USING

Theia - Table Lamp

The Greek goddess of light, Theia, as the name implies, expresses beauty and worship through the fusion of light and shadow. The lacquer material is matched with the smoky shade. Through the rotating function, it can be used for reading and pointing lighting, to feel the wonderful warm atmosphere, and to wander between light and shadow, enjoying the beauty of the shadows.

Urban - Wall Lamp

The Urban series simplifies the relationship between lamps and space, and creates a sense of hierarchy through the interlacing of neat horizontal and vertical lines with three-dimensional space. The brand's signature fog-black perforated metal lampshade creates both direct and indirect light sources, and with lightly polished brass decorative details, it presents an intellectual atmosphere of mature wash chains.

Aballs - Pendant

Jaime Hayón, known as the most unique designer and artist in Spain today, combines gorgeous materials with stunning visual effects, and paints the lamp body with lacquer metal ceramics, like a beautiful lady sitting in the air. The blown glass is white and flawless like the moon, giving the space a supremely beautiful vision.
